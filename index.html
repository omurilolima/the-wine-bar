About

Thinking about providing a complete gastronomic experience for wine lovers, Murilo Lima, a Brazilian who travels the world looking for the best tannins, decided to build The Wine Bar in the city of Dublin, Ireland.

The restaurant's proposal is to bring a select and seasonal menu, combining haute cuisine with the best wines from the four corners of the world. The Wine Bar is the ideal place for a special moment with the people we love.
